FamilyCompass

The privacy-first family coordination platform — encrypted GPS, calendar, messaging, calls, and health in one place.

  • Unlimited geofences with smart arrival & departure alerts
  • End-to-end encrypted messaging, voice calls, and video calls — up to 8 participants
  • Encrypted photo & document sharing with on-device OCR
  • Shared family calendar with AI conflict detection & Time Travel Engine
  • iOS Calendar, Google Calendar, and Outlook integration
  • Baby care: sleep, feeding, development milestone tracking
  • Elderly care: wandering detection, fall alerts, medication reminders
  • Wearable integration — 21+ devices including Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, Oura
  • Longevity nutrition, meal planning & auto grocery list generation
  • Pet care coordination, home task management, and budget tracking
  • 15-language support including RTL languages
  • Professional Deployment Mode for shift workers, military & aviation families

HealthCompass

HealthCompass

Encrypted patient-family communication for hospitals — HIPAA-compliant, staff-friendly, and designed to reduce the volume of incoming family phone calls.

For hospitals · ICUs · Maternity wards · NHS Trusts · Private clinics

  • One-tap status updates from clinical staff — "Stable", "Resting", "In Surgery", "Awake"
  • Mobile voice recording: on-device speech-to-text for fast ward notes
  • Photo OCR — capture handwritten charts, medication notes, wound photos
  • Encrypted push notifications to authorised family members only
  • Structured family update feed replacing repeated phone enquiries
  • Controlled visiting window notifications and booking coordination
  • Encrypted direct messaging between family and nursing station
  • REST API integration with Epic, Cerner, and Meditech (HL7 FHIR-compatible)
  • SSO via Active Directory / Azure AD for hospital IT compliance
  • Full HIPAA audit trail: timestamp, staff ID, content hash per §164.312(b)
  • Batch update mode for shift handover — update multiple families at once
  • Multi-language family app with offline mode and queued delivery
  • Department analytics: call reduction metrics, update frequency, CQC readiness reports

CareCompass

CareCompass

Encrypted family communication for nursing homes and residential care — giving families daily insight into their loved one's wellbeing without overburdening care staff.

For nursing homes · Residential care · Memory care · Home care agencies

  • Daily wellbeing updates: mood, appetite, activity level, social engagement
  • Mobile voice notes and OCR from bedside — minimal staff time required
  • Medication administration logging with timestamp and carer ID
  • Wound care and health observation photo logging — fully encrypted
  • GPS-based wandering detection with encrypted family alerts
  • Fall detection integration via wearables (Apple Watch, Samsung, Garmin)
  • Emergency contact cascade — encrypted alerts to family tiers in order
  • Activity tracking: meals, physiotherapy, social activities, faith activities
  • Visiting coordination and booking system for family members
  • Multi-family access with privacy partitions between residents
  • GP appointment notifications and encrypted health event sharing
  • CQC (UK) and HIQA (Ireland) inspection documentation generation
  • GDPR consent management with digital audit trail and right to erasure

EduCompass

EduCompass

GDPR-compliant encrypted communication for schools of all kinds — replacing fragmented WhatsApp groups, email chains, and paper letters with one secure platform.

For state schools · Private schools · International schools · Faith schools · Special schools · ISI / Ofsted regulated

  • Encrypted parent-teacher messaging with full audit trail
  • Class announcement broadcast with read-receipt analytics
  • Student progress updates with structured, searchable templates
  • Homework and assignment notifications with shared calendar sync
  • Parent evening scheduling with automated reminder workflows
  • Unified notification feed — all school communication in one place for parents
  • Child's school calendar auto-synced to parent's FamilyCompass
  • Absence reporting directly logged to school's management system
  • Payment and trip consent workflows with digital signatures
  • MIS connectors: Arbor, SIMS, iSAMS, Veracross — roster auto-sync
  • Multi-child support with separate, private threads per child
  • KCSIE safeguarding documentation and permission management
  • ISI / Ofsted inspection readiness reporting and parent engagement analytics
